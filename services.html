Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare services for the Nyagatare community

Medical Treatment

Our experienced physicians provide diagnosis and treatment for a wide range of illnesses and health conditions. We offer both general practice and specialized care with a patient-centered approach.

Laboratory Testing

Our modern laboratory is equipped to perform a comprehensive range of diagnostic tests including blood tests, urinalysis, microbiology, and more. We provide accurate and timely results to support your treatment.

Vaccination

We offer complete vaccination services for all ages, including routine childhood immunizations, travel vaccines, and seasonal flu shots. Our vaccines are properly stored and administered by trained professionals.

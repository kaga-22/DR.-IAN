About KIRA Clinic

Providing quality healthcare to Nyagatare since 2010

Our Story

KIRA Clinic was founded in 2010 with the mission to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare to the Nyagatare community. What began as a small practice has grown into a comprehensive health center serving thousands of patients each year.

Our name "KIRA" stands for "Knowledge, Integrity, Respect, and Accountability" - values that guide every aspect of our work. We believe in treating the whole person, not just the illness, and in building long-term relationships with our patients.

Over the years, we've invested in advanced medical equipment and continuous staff training to ensure we deliver the best possible care. Our team of dedicated healthcare professionals is committed to staying at the forefront of medical knowledge while maintaining the personal touch that makes us unique.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, high-quality healthcare services that meet the needs of our community through clinical excellence, innovative practices, and a commitment to continuous improvement.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider in Nyagatare District, recognized for exceptional patient care, medical expertise, and community health leadership.
